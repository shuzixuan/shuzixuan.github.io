Circurt Terminology and Kirchhoff’s Laws

1. Circuit Terminology

  • charge 电荷 (q) where n is a integer & $q_e = -1.602 \times 10^{-19}coulomb(C)$
  • current 电流 (i) unit: Ampere(A)
  • votage(potential) 电压(V)unit: volt(V)

 Note:
    (1) electric force only (energy $\uparrow$, $V_{AB}$ positive)
    (2) voltage is a relative quantity (Absolute quantity: reference ground or a point at infinity)
    (3) path of motion wires/circuit components
    (4) $V_{AB}$ independent of path

  • power 功率 (p) unit: walt(W)
    以上所有微分定义都可用积分进行运算

  • Ideal Voltage Source

 Note:

4. Kirchhoff’s Laws 基尔霍夫定律

  • Definition

    • Node（节点) : A point where two or more circuit elements are connected
    • Branch（支路) : A path that connects two nodes
    • Loop（ 环 ) : Any close path in the surface

  • Kirchhoff’s current Law (KCL)
      The algebraic sun of all the currents flowsing into (or out of) any node in a circuit equals to zero

Kirchhoff's current Law

 Proof :
     From law of consorvation of electric change（电荷守恒公理）

 Note:
    (1) reference direction is not actual direction of currents
    (2) in series(串联) $i_1 = i_2$ is a simple use of KCL
    (3) Any node → Any closed surface

  • Kirchhoff’s voltage Law (KVL)
      The algebraic sun of all the voltage(drop or rise) around any loop in a circuit equals to zero
Kirchhoff's voltage Law

 Proof :
     From law of consorvation of energy（能量守恒定律), energy supplied = energy consumed

 Note:
    (1) reference direction is not actual direction(polarity) of voltage
    (2) in parallel(并联) $V_1 = V_2$ is a simple use of KVL
